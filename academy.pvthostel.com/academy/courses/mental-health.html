Course Description

In the hospitality industry, we encounter guests from all walks of life, including those experiencing mental health challenges. This groundbreaking course equips you with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to provide compassionate support while maintaining safety for all.

Developed in collaboration with mental health professionals and experienced hostel operators, this course goes beyond theory to provide practical, real-world strategies you can implement immediately.

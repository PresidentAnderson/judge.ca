Frequently Asked Questions

How does the reverse auction work?

You post your project with a budget, and developers compete to offer the best solution within your price range. You choose the proposal that best fits your needs.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

All projects are protected by our escrow system. Funds are only released when you're completely satisfied. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Do I need technical knowledge?

Not at all! Describe your problem in plain language, and our AI helps translate it into technical requirements that developers can understand.

How long do projects typically take?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Simple automations can be done in days, while complex systems may take 1-2 months.

What happens after my project is complete?

You own all code and assets. Many clients choose to retain their developer for ongoing support, but it's entirely optional.

Can I see examples of completed projects?

Yes! Browse our success stories and completed projects to see real examples of what's been built for other hospitality businesses.

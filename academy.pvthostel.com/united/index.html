Our Declaration

The Manifesto

We Declare Our Independence

For too long, Online Travel Agencies have exploited our businesses, taking up to 30% of our hard-earned revenue while controlling our customer relationships and dictating our terms.

We say: NO MORE.

We are property owners, not slaves to corporate greed. We built these businesses with our sweat, tears, and dreams. The guests who stay with us come for OUR hospitality, not Booking.com's website.

Our Principles

  1. Direct Relationships: Our guests are OUR guests, not OTA customers
  2. Fair Pricing: We set our own rates without algorithmic manipulation
  3. Data Ownership: Guest information belongs to us, not corporations
  4. Financial Freedom: 100% of booking revenue stays with the property
  5. Collective Strength: United, we negotiate; divided, we beg
